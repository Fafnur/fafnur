What is this?

I wrote a short text quest in Ink, an interactive story engine. It's a tool used for indie games and narrative projects. I used it to make the introduction a little more lively.

Instead of a boring "about me" section, there's a dialogue. The system asks questions, you choose answers, and finally, you receive a profile. The branches change depending on who you are: HR, a developer, a blog reader, or just a casual visitor.
